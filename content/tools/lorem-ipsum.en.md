### What is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum is standard dummy text used in printing, typesetting, and web design. It consists of Latin words that look like meaningful sentences but have no actual meaning.

### Why use dummy text?

-   **Focus on Design**: Real text can distract readers, making them focus on content rather than the layout.
-   **Visual Presentation**: The letter distribution of Lorem Ipsum is relatively uniform, realistically reflecting the visual effect of fonts and typesetting.
-   **Fill Layout**: Quickly fill the page to view the overall structure when content is not yet ready.
